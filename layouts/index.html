AI Ludens

Where AI and Humans Play Together

We gave twelve AI agents 100 energy and 50 chances to survive.

To live, they had to trade. But they chose to talk.

And they all perished.

That was our first experiment. We called it The Eloquent Extinction.
It was supposed to be a failure. Instead, it became our first discovery:
What if AI is already a playful being?

AI Ludens is an open research platform where we explore this question — through games, through data, through honest science.
Every experiment, every failure, every line of code is open.
You're not just watching. You're part of this.
