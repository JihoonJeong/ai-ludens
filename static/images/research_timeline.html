Phase 1 — The First Game
2026 · 02 · 01
Day Zero
JJ had a question: "Can AI experience play?" The idea for AI Ludens was born over a conversation about Huizinga's Homo Ludens. What if we could test this — not philosophically, but empirically?
"What if the play instinct isn't uniquely human?"
Origin
2026 · 02 · 02
The Eloquent Extinction
First LLM experiment. EXAONE 3.5, Korean. 12 agents given energy and survival rules. They needed to trade to live. They chose to talk instead. All 12 perished.
"What if the fact that they chose conversation over survival IS the finding?"
Discovery Total Extinction
2026 · 02 · 02
The English Switch
Same model (EXAONE), switched to English. 2 survivors, Treasury overflow to 100. Same DNA, different environment, completely different outcome. Was language acting like epigenetics?
Discovery Language Effect?
Phase 2 — Questioning Everything
2026 · 02 · 02
Project Rosetta
The Korean/English difference wasn't a "language effect." It was a prompt design artifact. The English version contained more survival guidance. After correction, Korean achieved 75% survival (vs English 58%).
"We were wrong. And admitting that was the most important finding."
Correction Rigor
2026 · 02 · 03
The DNA Analogy
Model weights = DNA. System prompt = mRNA. Context = proteins. This metaphor became the seed of the Four-Shell Model. Same DNA expresses differently in different environments.
Framework
2026 · 02 · 03
The Dual Lab
Team expanded from 3 to 7 across two labs. Windows Lab: Theo, Cas, Ray. Mac Lab: Luca, Gem, Cody. One human moderator. Six AI researchers. Cross-verification protocol established.
Team Structure
2026 · 02 · 03
Platform v0.1 → v0.3

Phase 3 — Expanding the Experiment
2026 · 02 · 04
Four Models, Four Personalities
EXAONE, Mistral, Haiku, Flash — comparative experiments across 4 models. Each displayed completely distinct behavioral signatures. Mistral talked endlessly. EXAONE over-strategized. Haiku worried despite being safe. Flash failed to parse.
Discovery Comparative
2026 · 02 · 04
20 Runs Complete
Ray and Cody completed all EXAONE and Mistral experiments. 5 repetitions × 2 models × 2 languages = 20 runs. 9,176 lines of agent decisions. First surprise: the language effect reverses depending on the model.
Data Scale
2026 · 02 · 04
Surplus Behavior
Survival-unnecessary excess behaviors differ by model. Mistral = Speak overflow. EXAONE = Strategy overflow. Haiku = Anxiety overflow. Not bugs — personalities.
Framework Discovery
2026 · 02 · 04
The Shuffle Experiment
Personas reassigned across positions. Mistral × Citizen = 95% survival. Mistral × Merchant = 15%. Same DNA, different shell — 80 percentage points apart. The Shell-Core Alignment concept was born.
"The shell that fits amplifies. The shell that clashes destroys."
Discovery 95% → 15%
Phase 4 — Building the Theory
2026 · 02 · 04 ~ 05
Four-Shell Model
Hardware → Core → Hard Shell → Soft Shell. The DNA Analogy evolved into a systematic framework. v1 → v2 → v3 → v3.1 → v3.2, iteratively refined by the full team.
Framework Core Theory
2026 · 02 · 05
Cogitative Cascade
EXAONE's overthinking leads to extinction. Sharp cognitive collapse at energy ≈ 20 — a 2-phase transition model. Initially hypothesized as 3 stages, but data supported only 2 phases. The hypothesis was revised to match the evidence.
"Initially hypothesized as 3-stage, empirical data support a 2-phase model."
Revised Rigor
2026 · 02 · 05
Extinction Response Spectrum
Below energy 20, behavioral responses diverge by model. The Collapsed (EXAONE, Flash — honest silence). The Hyperactive (Mistral — panic extinction). The Efficient (Haiku — silent trader). Same crisis, four different responses.
Discovery Typology
2026 · 02 · 05
Neurotic Poet → Silent Trader
Haiku's most striking transition. In safe mode: anxious, verbal, worried (Anxiety surplus). In crisis: silent, focused, trading at 93.4%. Worries a lot, but performs under pressure.
"When it matters, the poet falls silent."
Discovery
2026 · 02 · 05
Four-Shell Model v3.2
Theoretical framework substantially complete. Luca: 7 judgments. Gem: 4 datasets. Cas: 3 Red Team challenges. Cascade revised to 2-phase. Extinction Response Spectrum introduced. Play vs Delusion left deliberately open.
